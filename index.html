Game Rules

  • Sudoku is a 9×9 grid divided into 9 subgrids (boxes) of 3×3
  • The goal is to fill the grid with numbers 1-9 so that each row, column, and box contains all numbers 1-9 without repetition
  • Pre-filled numbers (gray background) are fixed and cannot be changed
  • Use the number pad or keyboard to enter numbers in empty cells
  • Conflicting numbers will be highlighted in red

Why Play Sudoku Daily?

Improves Concentration

Solving Sudoku puzzles requires focused attention, which helps train your brain to concentrate better in other areas of life.

Enhances Logical Thinking

Sudoku challenges your deductive reasoning skills, teaching you to approach problems methodically and logically.

Reduces Stress

The focused nature of Sudoku serves as a form of meditation, helping to clear your mind and reduce anxiety.

Prevents Cognitive Decline

Regular mental exercise like Sudoku helps maintain brain health and may delay age-related cognitive decline.
